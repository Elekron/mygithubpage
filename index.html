Me

I am a creative social Interaction Designer with interests such as:

Programming - coding all day long to see at the end a working result are the thing that make me pleased.

Photography - Being able to capture a perspective that no one else is able to do is what i love with taking pictures.

Psychology - People are unique! Is that why everyone has an apple product?

Training - My version of taking a creative break.

Past

I have studied three years engineering at high school and at this time I never dreamt that I would one day like to code. It so happened that my interest back then was to design.

When I applied to Umeå University I always had in mind that I would study something with design and I did, just not in the way I imagined. It was actually how to code and design programs that every day human can understand.

This education gave me a new perspective on how the new world works.

Future

To develop as a person, build on new knowledge. Both in programming and in the mechanical world. Then I want to be able to find great solutions to everyday problems. But of course, I want to create something unique that will be remembered.
